Chicken Adobo

Ingredients

  • Chicken thigh (with skin on)
  • Garlic
  • Bay leaf
  • Soy sauce
  • White vinegar

Instructions

  1. Peel and smash garlic
  2. Combine soy sauce, vinegar, and bay leaf in a pot
  3. Salt and pepper chicken thighs
  4. Heat oil in a pan and sear chicken thighs until brown around edges
  5. Transfer chicken to pot and simmer in sauce until cooked
  6. Let cool and enjoy!
