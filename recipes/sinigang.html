Sinigang

Ingredients

  • Sinigang Soup Base
  • Shrimp
  • Vegetables

Instructions

  1. Peel shrimp
  2. Bring water to a boil, and combine with soup base
  3. Put shrimp in pot for half normal cooking time
  4. Place vegetables of choice in pot until all ingredients are fully cooked
  5. Let cool and enjoy!
