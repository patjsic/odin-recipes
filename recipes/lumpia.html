Lumpia

Ingredients

  • Egg wrapper
  • Beef
  • Pork
  • Mung beans
  • Garlic

Instructions

  1. Mince garlic
  2. Combine garlic, beef, pork, and mung beans into filling
  3. Place filling into egg wrapper and close
  4. Heat oil in a pan and fry lumpia until golden brown in color
  5. Let cool and enjoy!
